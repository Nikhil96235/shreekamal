OUR STORY

A Legacy of Purity,
Trust & Growth

1969 — THE BEGINNING
Sh. Suresh Singhal Ji's family begins its journey in Cattle Feed & Oil Trading — laying the foundation of trust and quality that would define the group for generations.
2011 — SHREEKAMAL OIL INDUSTRIES FOUNDED
Building on 40+ years of legacy, Shreekamal Agro is formally established at Hinganghat, Maharashtra — scaling operations with modern milling technology.
2015–2020 — EXPANSION ACROSS INDIA
Group expands operations — Aadit Oil Mill and Singhal Agro Products established. Network grows to 5+ states with multiple brand launches including Radha Govind, Panchratan & Radha Kaithal.
TODAY — ₹300 CRORE+ TURNOVER
10 Lakh+ happy clients across 7 states. Consistent growth through quality products and timely deliveries — and the journey continues.
